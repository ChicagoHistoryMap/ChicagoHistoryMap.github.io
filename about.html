About Chicago History Map

A Passion Project of Digital Vintage Exploration
built by Vagabond Studios

ChicagoHistoryMap.org is a visual tour of historical maps & imagery sourced from a variety of university and library archives.
This site aims to combine these various images and present them in a manner to convey a sense of place and history through a visual tour of the past. Feel free to reach out with any questions, corrections, contributions, or just to say Hello.
